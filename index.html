CEO-first methodology

We prioritize leadership fit and incentive alignment, then execute the acquisition with disciplined underwriting and governance.

  1. Define the ideal CEO profile and success criteria
  2. Source & vet candidates through a structured process
  3. Match CEO + target business
  4. Align incentives via meaningful equity participation
  5. Operate with a clear cadence, KPIs, and board governance
