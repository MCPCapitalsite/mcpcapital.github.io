What we are

MCP Capital is a Search Fund / long-term acquisition vehicle focused on majority acquisitions of high-quality SMEs.

We aim for discreet, founder-friendly processes, pairing strong businesses with the right leaders and a pragmatic execution model.

What we are

  • Long-term owner (10+ year mindset)
  • Majority acquirer (control where needed)
  • Operator-led governance and execution cadence

What we are not

  • Not a broker / intermediary
  • Not an opportunistic “flip” fund
  • Not a consulting firm

Positioning

A strategic bridge between owners and CEOs: we identify strong SMEs and match them with proven operators to drive long-term performance.

How we work

The process is two-way: we select the right operator for the right business — and we select the right business for the right operator.

Target → CEO

Matching & integration

  • Match operator strengths to business needs
  • Align incentives (meaningful equity)
  • Transition plan + governance cadence
  1. Identify a high-quality SME aligned with our criteria
  2. Engage discreetly and align on confidentiality + intent
  3. Structure LOI → diligence → financing
  4. Close with clear governance and incentives
  5. Execute a 90-day plan and KPI rhythm post-acquisition
