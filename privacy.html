Where required by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your personal data on one of the following bases:

  • Legal obligation — where we are required to comply with applicable law.

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, email) who process it on our behalf under appropriate data-processing agreements. We may also disclose data where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by applicable law.
