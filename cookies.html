Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device and store certain information about your preferences or actions.

2. Cookies We Use

This website uses only the following categories of cookies:

Category Purpose Required
Strictly necessary Enable core website functionality (navigation, security, session management). Yes
Preference Remember user preferences (e.g. display settings) across visits. No
Analytics Collect aggregated, anonymous data on how visitors use the site to help us improve it. No

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies, and we do not share cookie data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Your Consent

Strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent. For all other cookies, we will ask for your consent before placing them on your device. You may withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or by using our cookie preference tool (if available).

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit www.aboutcookies.org.

5. Further Information

For more information about how we handle personal data collected via cookies, please refer to our Privacy Policy. For any questions, contact us via the contact form on our website.
